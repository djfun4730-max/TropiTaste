Mango Pudding

A Sweet Mango Dessert With A Creamy Tropical Twist

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp gelatin or agar-agar
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • Mint leaves or mango slices (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Blend chopped mangoes into a smooth purée.
  2. Dissolve gelatin in hot water and set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, heat milk, sugar, and cream until warm (don’t boil).
  4. Add mango purée and mix well.
  5. Stir in the dissolved gelatin and mix again.
  6. Pour into bowls or cups and chill in the fridge for 4 hours.
  7. Garnish with mint or mango slices before serving.
