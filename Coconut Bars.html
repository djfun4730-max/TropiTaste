Chocolate Coconut Dream Bars 🥥

Chewy coconut treats dipped in chocolate drizzle.

🥥 Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs (about 8 full crackers, finely crushed)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups shredded sweetened coconut
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Line an 8x8-inch or 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving overhang for easy removal.
  3. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, brown sugar, and melted butter until evenly combined.
  4. Press mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 8 minutes, then remove from oven.
  6. In a mixing bowl, stir together shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla extract until fully combined.
  7. Spread evenly over the baked crust.
  8. Return to oven and bake for 15–18 minutes, or until edges start to turn golden.
  9. Let cool completely.
  10. In a heatproof bowl, melt chocolate chips and butter together (microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring each time, or use a double boiler).
  11. Pour over cooled coconut layer and spread evenly.
  12. Refrigerate for at least 1–2 hours until chocolate is firm.
  13. Lift bars from pan using parchment paper and cut into squares with a sharp knife.
